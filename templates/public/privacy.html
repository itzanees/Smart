Privacy Policy for Smart Hospital

Last Updated: 21-January-2025

1. Introduction

Smart Hospital ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our website (the "Site") and our online services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This may include your name, address, phone number, email address, date of birth, insurance information, and medical history. We collect this when you register an account, book an appointment, or contact us.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our Site, such as the pages you visit, the links you click, and the time you spend on each page.
  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record information ("log data"), including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring website.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activity. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Schedule and manage appointments.
  • Communicate with you about your appointments and other important information.
  • Personalize your experience on our Site.
  • Analyze website usage and trends.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your care.
  • Third-party service providers who assist us with website hosting, data analysis, and other services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and secure server infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You may:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information by contacting us.
  • Opt out of receiving marketing emails from us.
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings (this may affect your experience on our Site).

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and encourage you to review it periodically.
