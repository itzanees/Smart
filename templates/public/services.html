Health Checkup - She

The Women’s Health Checkup Package provides a thorough screening focused on female-specific health concerns. It includes a general physical examination, blood tests such as a complete blood count (CBC), blood sugar, lipid profile, and thyroid function tests. The package also includes a Pap smear for cervical cancer screening, a mammogram for breast health (for women over 40), and a pelvic ultrasound for reproductive health. Additionally, the package includes bone density testing for osteoporosis, an electrocardiogram (ECG) for heart health, and consultations with both gynecologists and nutritionists to ensure comprehensive care.

Health Checkup - Senior Citizen

The Senior Citizen Health Checkup Package is designed specifically for older adults, offering a comprehensive evaluation of aging-related health risks. It includes a general physical examination, blood tests for overall health, kidney and liver function tests, a cholesterol test, a bone density test for osteoporosis, and a heart health assessment with an electrocardiogram (ECG). The package also includes a chest X-ray, vision and hearing tests, and a memory function evaluation to monitor cognitive health. Additionally, consultations with geriatric specialists ensure that all concerns related to aging are addressed.
